FORGIVENESS vs FORGETNESS

chandra999 • Jul 4, 2022 • 1 min read

Many people say that forgiveness is an act which gives peacefulness to an individual and retains relationships.

But FORGIVENESS NATURE IS DIRECTLY RELATED TO FORGETNESS NATURE.

A person with forgetness nature will forget everybody — the people who harmed him and the people who helped him. So he does not have any grudge on anybody and also he does not have any gratitude towards the people who helped him.

But the people with memory cannot forget the people who harmed him and also cannot forget who helped him. Because of this he cannot forgive the people who harmed him and will be grateful towards people who helped him.

People with memory do not have peace.

So sometimes FORGETNESS IS A BOON.
